About Us

Your Best Spa, Beauty & Skin Care Therapist

Our CEO, Chinenye, is a certified massage therapist with over 5 years of experience She is passionate about helping people feel better and achieve their wellness goals. Our CEO started Jas Spa & Massage with the goal of creating a spa that would provide a relaxing and luxurious experience for her clients.

We are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality service.

Our services are not limited to our spa location. We also offer home services. This means that you can enjoy a relaxing massage or other treatment in the comfort of your own home. Our home services are perfect for people who are busy, have mobility issues, or simply prefer to be pampered in their own home.
